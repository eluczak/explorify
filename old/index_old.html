Your Spotify listening stats

Here you can discover your listening habits and music preferences based on your streaming history in Spotify.

To do this you need to request your last year's listening history from Spotify website (you find this option in the privacy settings). Usually after 2 or 3 days the data is available to download and you will receive a set of files containing various information that has been collected. The file we need contains only listening records and is named StreamingHistory0.json.

Just upload the file in the field on the left and enjoy!



NOTE: After upload please wait a while. It may take up to one minute to load. Please also note that tracks that were played shorter than 0.5 min will be excluded of this report to avoid taking into account accidentally played songs.
